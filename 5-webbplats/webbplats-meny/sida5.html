Wilbur Soot

Will Gold (born: September 14, 1996 [age 24]), better known online as Wilbur Soot, is a British YouTuber best known for being the frontman and editor of the group YouTube channel SootHouse and has also edited for fellow Youtuber JackSucksAtLife before. He is a rising solo YouTuber and Twitch streamer, as well as a musician. He has released an album on Spotify and has made a few viral parody songs such as "Karen, please come back I miss the kids" and "The 'Nice Guy' Ballad." Currently, he mainly does live streams on Twitch and then posts edited highlights onto YouTube.
